About our project

Motivation & Rationale

Food safety is a top priority for any community wishing to keep its residents safe and healthy. According to the World Health Organization, 600 million people become ill from contaminated food, and ~420,000 people die every year1.

Currently, there is no easily accessible and intuitive way to determine if the places they go to are compliant with food inspections.

We hope to solve this problem with this web application.
