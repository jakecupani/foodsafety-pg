About our project

Motivation & Rationale

Food safety is a top priority for any community wishing to keep its residents safe and healthy. According to the World Health Organization, 600 million people become ill from contaminated food, and ~420,000 people die every year1. This web application is targeted to those who lack easy access to tools to allow them to make well-informed healthy eating decisions. Prince George’s county has the 3rd highest number of households in Maryland yet 12th in Median Household Income2. While other counties may have more money, the residents of Prince George’s county lack the same resources. We believe everyone deserves to know what exactly is going into their body, and if that's going to be safe. This is why we've create PGCFI.

Currently, there is no easily accessible and intuitive way to determine compliant restaurants and eateries.

We hope this web application will help those in PG county easily get food safety results to make better informed eating decisions.
